Our Four Pillars

Built around what matters most on site.

Every screen, every workflow and every report in Safety Digi is shaped by these four pillars — so nothing gets missed in the field.

Safety

Standardize incident reporting, near-miss capture and corrective actions. Auto-generate shareable PDF reports the moment an event is logged.

Health

Track injuries, medical services and lost working days. Capture LTI data with structured forms so health trends surface instantly.

Environment

Log environmental incidents and inspections with photo evidence. Spot patterns early and keep sustainability on the daily agenda.

People

From daily Toolbox Talks to manpower and manhour tracking — give every worker on site a voice and a record.

Four Core Modules

One app. Every HSE workflow.

From the moment a worker spots something on site to the monthly report on the manager's desk — Safety Digi covers it.

Module 01

Observation

Log a safety observation in seconds — type, place, category, time to close, responsible person — and let supervisors track close-outs through history and trend stats.

  • Quick dropdown-driven form (Type / Place / Category / Responsible)
  • Photo evidence on every record
  • Sortable history per author
  • Stats: Negative, Positive, Closed + Trend
Module 02

Inspection

Six structured inspection types, each with a tailored Yes / No / N/A checklist, an "Other (specify)" field and photo attach — built for gloves, outdoors, and one-handed use.

Equipment PPE Scaffold Environmental Fire SMS Audit (Phase 2)
Module 03

Reports

Daily reports capture manpower, manhours, TBTs and Permit-to-Work status. Once 26 daily reports land, the Monthly Report auto-builds. Incident reports export to PDF in a single tap.

  • Daily — Manpower · Manhour · TBT · Attendees · PWO Pending
  • Monthly — auto-calculated from 26 daily reports
  • Incident — Near miss · Accident · Environmental · LTI
  • PDF export ready to share with stakeholders
Module 04

Monthly Stats

A management-grade performance view — aggregated HSE metrics that turn 26 days of daily field activity into clear, comparable monthly numbers.

  • Observation trend over time
  • Open vs Closed vs Incident counts
  • TBT, Manhour and PWO rollups
  • Drill down by site, area or role

How It Works

From field observation to monthly report — in four steps.

01

Capture

Field worker taps the center camera button or opens a module. Picklists + photo evidence — minimal typing.

02

Submit

Each record is stamped with Author, Date/Time and Level — automatically routed to the right supervisor.

03

Track

Open vs Closed, Positive vs Negative, trends by week. Corrective actions stay visible until confirmed.

04

Report

26 daily reports auto-roll into a monthly report. Incident PDFs are one tap away.
